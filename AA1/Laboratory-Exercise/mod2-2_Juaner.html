Horimiya

A story of a popular girl named Kyoko Hori who met and fell in love with an very introverted and quiet guy named Izumi Miyamura. It is an anime filled with some slice of life, romance, and comedy with various side characters that make the anime feel alive, colorful, and real.

My Top 5 Favorite Characters

  1. Kyoko Hori
  2. Izumi Miyamura
  3. Sakura Kono
  4. Toru Ishikawa
  5. Honoko Sawada
